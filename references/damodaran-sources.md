## Current-Data Rule

For market prices, rates, ERP, CRP, default spreads, beta, peer multiples, and public-company financials, browse or otherwise verify current data. Always report the data date.

For stable conceptual issues, local books and class notes are enough. For live valuation inputs, they are not enough.

## Citation Standard

Use concise source labels:

- `Damodaran, Investment Valuation, ch. X`
- `Damodaran, The Dark Side of Valuation, ch. X`
- `Damodaran current data page, accessed YYYY-MM-DD`
- `Company filing, FY/YYYY 10-K, p. X`
- `User-provided model/file, tab/section X`

If a conclusion is not directly stated by a source, label it as an inference.
